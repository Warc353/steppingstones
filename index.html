Welcome to Stepping Stones!

Stepping Stones is a licensed, nonprofit preschool and daycare located in Farmville, VA. At Stepping Stones, our goal is to provide high-quality, affordable programs for children ages 6 weeks to 12 years in a safe, "play and learn" environment that stimulates and nurtures children's academic, social, emotional, and physical growth.

  • We are fully licensed according to Department of Social Services Standards by the Commonwealth of Virginia.
  • Our small class sizes and low student-teacher ratios allow for us to provide individual attention and customized care to meet your child's needs.
  • Our teachers have over 40 years combined professional childcare experience.
  • We are a nonprofit organization, governed by a Board of Directors composed of the parents of Stepping Stones' students and alumni. Parents who--just like you--want to ensure the highest quality learning and care environment for their children.

For more information, come visit us at our school, located near the Fuqua School at 821 Longwood Avenue, contact us via phone: (434) 392-1738 or (540) 455-0384 or email our Director, Samantha Edwards.

Schedule/Daily Activities

Because each classroom follows an individualized schedule appropriate to the developmental level of the students, this schedule represents a rough outline of the daily activities.

  • 7:15-9:00: Free play time: children may eat breakfast, do puzzles, visit centers, color or read books
  • 9:00-9:15: Snack time (snack is included in your tuition)
  • 9:15-9:45: Circle time: we sing our welcome song, say the pledge, movement, calendar learning, weather, and review the theme for the week.
  • 9:45-11:00: Table time: we paint, color, talk, read, learn letters, colors, numbers and shape recognition.
  • 11:00-11:30: Controlled center play: Children learn to stay in one center and play with a partner(s) until the teacher asks them to switch.
  • 11:30-12:00: Lunch time
  • 12:00-1:00: Free play/center time/outdoor play
  • 1:00-3:00: Nap/rest/quiet time: children will nap to classical music
  • 3:00-3:15: Afternoon snack
  • 3:15-5:30: Free play/center time/outside play
  • 5:30-6:00: Evening snack/combined groups/small group centers
